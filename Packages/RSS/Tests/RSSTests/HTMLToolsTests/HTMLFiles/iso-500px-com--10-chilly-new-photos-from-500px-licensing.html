10 chilly new photos from 500px Licensing Contributors

Published by Devon Steele • 2 weeks ago

Experienced commercial photographers understand that landscape photography serves as a cornerstone in commercial Licensing. Stunning images featuring diverse landscapes continue to prove invaluable for tapping into popular commercial themes, such as adventure, travel, escape, wanderlust, and sustainability.





Keeping your Licensing portfolio fresh with seasonal content is a great strategy for maintaining the saleability of your landscape and nature photography. Continuously producing new material with each season not only fosters your creative growth but also builds a versatile reservoir of content that retains its market appeal over time.



Persistently capturing fresh scenes as you advance in your photography journey enables you to refine your style, explore new aspects of nature and landscape photography, and incorporate trending keywords into your metadata. This approach ensures that your content remains attuned to evolving social and commercial trends, and the terms content buys use when searching for new photos to license.
